SPRINT 005 — Interface de Conteúdo no Filament

1) Envie este ZIP para:
~/vitrine-ai-social-enterprise

2) No terminal:
cd ~/vitrine-ai-social-enterprise
unzip -o SPRINT_005_FILAMENT_CONTENT_UI.zip

php artisan route:list | grep -i content
